Advanced Smelting Solutions

We operate state-of-the-art induction and arc furnaces capable of processing:
  • Doré bars (0-99% purity)
  • Jewelry/industrial scrap
  • E-waste concentrates
  • Custom alloy blends

    • Specialty Smelting Services
  • High-volume production: Up to 5-ton batches for mining partners
  • Low-volume precision: 100g micro-batches for R&D/labs
  • Alloy formulation: Custom karat gold, silver alloys, etc.

    • Environmental Responsibility:
    While we harness the power of smelting to transform metals, we do so with a commitment to environmental responsibility. Our smelting processes adhere to stringent environmental standards, minimizing our ecological footprint and contributing to sustainable practices.

    Quality Assurance:
    Quality is the bedrock of our smelting services. Rigorous quality assurance protocols are integrated into every step of the smelting journey, from raw material intake to the casting of refined alloys. This commitment to quality guarantees that our clients receive products of uncompromising excellence.

    Timely Deliveries:
    At Quantum Refineries, we recognize that time is money in the metals industry. That's why our optimized smelting processes are designed for maximum speed without compromising quality. We guarantee rapid processing and dependable delivery timelines, ensuring your refined materials arrive precisely when needed to keep your operations running smoothly.
